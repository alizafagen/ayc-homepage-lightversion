Commercial Real Estate & Office Buildings

Protecting the Buildings Where Business Happens

Houses of Worship & Religious Communities

Security for Communities Built on Trust

AYC Security provides professional security for synagogues, churches, mosques, and religious campuses, with active deployments in Florida and Texas covering regular weekly services, High Holiday events, and year-round programming.

Security for houses of worship carries a responsibility that goes beyond access control. Communities need to feel protected, not surveilled. AYC officers are trained to maintain a professional, visible presence that supports the community's sense of safety while managing access, coordinating with local law enforcement when needed, and responding decisively when threats arise.

AYC's experience with religious communities spans routine Shabbat and Sunday coverage, high-attendance holiday events, visiting speaker engagements, and long-term site security programs. Our presence in both Florida and Texas continues to expand as organizations across denominations prioritize active security planning.

Healthcare & Medical Facilities

Security That Understands How Healthcare Works

AYC provides security for hospitals, clinics, and specialized medical facilities, with leadership credentials that include a dedicated Vice President of Healthcare Operations and a Vice President of Medical Training who is a practicing emergency physician with trauma center experience.

Healthcare security requires more than a uniformed presence. Officers need to understand patient sensitivity, HIPAA-adjacent environment protocols, and the access control challenges unique to facilities that operate continuously and serve vulnerable populations. AYC's security programs are designed around how healthcare facilities actually function, not adapted from a general commercial template.

For healthcare organizations evaluating vendors, staff, or contractors, AYC Intelligence provides background investigations and due diligence services reviewed by in-house legal counsel.

Energy, Mining & Industrial Sites

Built for Operations That Cannot Stop

AYC provides specialized security for active mining operations, energy facilities, ports, warehouses, factories, and industrial sites, with confirmed operations in Nevada and Ohio and leadership that includes professionals with direct mining and industrial experience.

Industrial security demands officers who understand the operational realities of the site, shift structure, equipment access controls, perimeter integrity across large footprints, and the difference between a credentialed vendor and an unauthorized visitor. AYC's approach is built around those realities, not adapted from a standard commercial security template.

Every AYC industrial deployment is GSOC-monitored, with GPS-confirmed hourly check-ins and immediate response protocols designed for complex and remote environments. Armed and unarmed options are available depending on site requirements.

Events & Conferences

Professional Security for Every Type of Event

AYC provides event security for galas, conferences, conventions, corporate gatherings, and private functions, with active deployments in Florida and Ohio and same-day coverage available for urgent requests.

AYC officers maintain a professional presence calibrated to the environment, a black-tie gala requires a different bearing than a multi-day conference, and both require something different from a community event.
