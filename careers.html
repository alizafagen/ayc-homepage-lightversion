Why Join AYC?

AYC Security strives to be the finest mid-market full-service security firm in the world. The only way we can do that is with the best personnel.

  • Competitive wages and comprehensive benefits
  • Extensive training and career growth opportunities
  • Flexible scheduling across multiple shifts
  • Supportive and collaborative team environment
  • Advancement into leadership and supervisory roles
  • Access to AYC's Global Security Operations Center technology

What We Look For

AYC holds its officers to a higher standard. Our clients depend on it. Here is what we look for in every candidate.

  • Valid security guard license, or willingness to obtain one. Florida requires a Class D (unarmed) or Class G (armed) license.
  • Valid driver's license and access to a personal vehicle.
  • Military or law enforcement background preferred. Our leadership team is built from both communities, and we actively recruit from veterans and former officers.
  • Professional presentation and demeanor. AYC officers represent the firm at every deployment.
  • Comfort with accountability. Every AYC officer checks in hourly through our Global Security Operations Center. This is how we protect our clients and our people.
  • Physical fitness for patrol and incident response. Clear background check required.

Veterans & Law Enforcement

AYC Security is veteran-owned and actively recruits from the military and law enforcement community. If you have served in any branch or capacity, we want to hear from you.
