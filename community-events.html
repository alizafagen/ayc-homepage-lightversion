Not every security situation looks the same. A synagogue on Shabbat needs a presence that is protective but welcoming. A gala needs officers who are professional, discreet, and suited to the environment. An executive moving through a high-profile event needs a different level of attention entirely.

AYC provides security that fits the event. Armed and unarmed. Uniformed and plainclothes. Same-day deployment available for time-sensitive event needs. Every deployed officer is monitored through our Global Security Operations Center with GPS-confirmed hourly check-ins.

Community Security

Where community gathers,
we stand watch.

Houses of worship, schools, and community organizations carry a responsibility to the people who gather there. AYC provides security that protects that responsibility without disrupting the warmth and openness that defines those spaces.

Our officers are trained for environments where the tone of the security presence matters as much as the preparation behind it. We have extensive experience with religious communities — synagogues, churches, mosques — and the specific security considerations each community faces.

  • Shabbat, High Holiday, and year-round synagogue security
  • Church, mosque, and multi-faith community security
  • School and campus security
  • Community events with speakers or large gatherings
  • Access control and perimeter monitoring
  • GSOC-monitored hourly officer check-ins
