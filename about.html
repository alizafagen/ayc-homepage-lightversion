Built by people who know what is at stake

AYC Security was built around a simple belief: the best security thinking comes from people who have done the hard work in the real world. Our founding team came from careers in military special operations, law enforcement, criminal law, and psychotherapy. That breadth was intentional. Those varied backgrounds create a depth of perspective that benefits every client we serve, with a team dedicated to providing the very best in physical security, training, and consulting.

Today we work with organizations that need security done properly and consistently. Commercial office buildings, hospitals, ports, energy facilities, schools, houses of worship, and residential communities. We bring the same level of preparation and care to a long-term site contract as we do to a same-day emergency deployment.

AYC Security USA organizational structure. The President oversees six direct reports: Chief Financial Officer, Chief Operating Officer, General Counsel, Chief Personnel Officer, Chief of Staff, and Chief Marketing Officer. The Chief Operating Officer oversees three directorates: Consulting Directorate including Crisis Management Division, Threat Assessment Division, Risk Analysis Division, and Penetration Testing Division; Security Directorate including Transportation Hubs, Hospitals, Large Commercial Entities, and Mines and Construction Sites; and Training Directorate including Workplace Safety, Leadership and Decision-Making, and Communication and Legalities. The Chief Personnel Officer oversees the Admin Directorate including Human Resources Office, Logistics Division, and Procurement Office; and the Education Directorate. The Chief Marketing Officer oversees Government Sales, Individual Sales, and Commercial Sales.
