What Is the GSOC?

Our Global Security Operations Center

At the center of AYC's operation is the Global Security Operations Center, a 24/7 monitoring function that ensures every officer at every post is confirmed present and responsive. Staffed by live operators around the clock, it runs every hour of every day of the year.

GPS Monitoring

Every deployed officer checks in with GPS confirmation every hour, on the hour. Coverage begins the moment an officer takes a post.

Live Human Oversight

A live person tracks and reviews every check-in. We don't rely on an automated software system that can glitch, flag exceptions after the fact, or be missed by managing personnel.

Immediate Response

Through live monitoring, our team can notify you and respond to any incidents immediately. This is the AYC difference.

Why It Matters

What Sets Our Operations Center Apart

The private security industry has no universal standard for officer verification. Some firms rely on self-reported check-ins or automated software that flags exceptions only after they occur. AYC takes a different approach.

AYC's GSOC was built around a straightforward premise: a client who has engaged a security firm should not have to wonder whether their officers are actually on post. GPS check-ins, verified by a live human operator every hour, provide that answer.

For clients with legal, insurance, or compliance exposure, this distinction is material. Every monitored post generates documentation that can support an incident response, a legal proceeding, or an internal review.
