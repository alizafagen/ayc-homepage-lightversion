Consulting

Security Built
For Your Program

From threat assessments and penetration testing to crisis planning and emergency response frameworks, AYC's consulting team builds programs grounded in operational reality.

Our team has operated in environments ranging from deployed military operations to nuclear site security. Every recommendation we deliver is tested against real-world conditions, not academic standards. In-house legal counsel is integrated into every engagement.

Our consulting engagements include: managing costs and risks, developing integrated security solutions, cooperating with and optimizing your existing systems, redesigning protocols to meet current industry standards, and where applicable, identifying and leveraging available government funding mechanisms.

Training

Arm Your Team
For Every Threat

AYC's training courses help clients create safer, more productive working environments. Whether a corporate leadership team, a security staff at a retail complex, or a nonprofit organization, every program is built on the latest lessons from military, law enforcement, and private security practice, filtered for civilian applicability.

Two things set AYC training apart. First, rigorous analysis ensures every course fits your organization's specific context, culture, and regulatory environment. Second, every program is reviewed by our in-house legal counsel so it is legally and ethically sound, reducing the liability concerns organizations rightly have when procuring training.

We offer courses in one, two, and three-day formats, delivered in-person and virtually. Custom programs are available.

Leadership & Decision-Making

A Distinct Program. The Same Standard.

AYC's Leadership and Decision-Making program is a dedicated curriculum developed for executives, security leaders, and organizational teams. Built on proven military and law enforcement instructional methods, it covers decision games, critical thinking, adult learning theory, curriculum design, and high-stakes communication.
