Mining Operations

Mining sites present unique security challenges. Large and often remote perimeters. High-value equipment and extracted materials. Personnel working around the clock in conditions where a coverage gap creates real exposure.

AYC has direct experience providing security for active mining operations, including operations in Nevada. Our senior leadership brings firsthand knowledge of mining environments. We understand the operational rhythm of these sites and build security coverage around it.

  • Perimeter security for large and remote sites
  • Equipment and materials protection
  • Personnel access control
  • Armed officers for high-value asset areas
  • GSOC-monitored hourly check-ins
  • Incident documentation

Ports, Maritime Facilities & Rail

Maritime ports, cargo facilities, and rail yards are critical infrastructure and high-value targets for theft, unauthorized access, and disruption. AYC provides security personnel experienced in port environments, supporting access control, cargo protection, perimeter monitoring, and incident response.

Our security approach aligns with compliance frameworks including the International Ship and Port Facility Security Code (ISPS) and the Customs-Trade Partnership Against Terrorism (C-TPAT). Our integrated legal counsel provides the regulatory awareness that port security demands.

  • Access control and identity management
  • Cargo and vehicle screening support
  • Perimeter and waterside monitoring
  • ISPS and C-TPAT compliance awareness
  • Incident documentation and response
  • Armed and unarmed officer deployment

Energy Facilities & Utilities

Power plants, pipelines, and utility infrastructure require continuous, protocol-driven security. These facilities operate under strict regulatory frameworks, including requirements from the Nuclear Regulatory Commission and the Department of Energy, and cannot afford a security incident.

AYC provides armed and unarmed security personnel experienced in regulated facility environments, supported by GSOC monitoring and integrated legal counsel. We work with your compliance and safety teams to ensure our security approach meets your regulatory obligations.

  • Armed and unarmed security for regulated facilities
  • Access control and identity management
  • Perimeter protection and patrol
  • NRC and DOE compliance awareness
  • Emergency response coordination
  • Incident documentation and regulatory reporting

Factories, Warehouses & Industrial Facilities

Manufacturing facilities and warehouses face significant exposure to theft, unauthorized access, and vandalism, particularly overnight and on weekends. From single-site factories to multi-location warehouse networks, we build security coverage that matches your operation.
